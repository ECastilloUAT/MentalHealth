Mental Health News



ScienceDaily

Science Daily's mental health section covers the latest research findings in the field of mental health and neuroscience. It includes studies related to conditions such as depression, anxiety, and PTSD, along with insights into treatments, therapies, and brain science.



NBC

NBC News' mental health section provides news, stories, and updates about mental health issues, trends, and research. It covers topics such as mental health awareness, treatment options, the impact of societal factors, and personal stories. The section also includes expert opinions and insights on managing mental health challenges.



NPR

NPR's mental health section provides in-depth coverage of mental health issues, featuring stories about the challenges individuals face, interviews with experts, and discussions of societal impacts on mental health. Topics include coping strategies, new research findings, personal stories, and mental health policy.



NIH

The NIMH Science News on Treatments section provides the latest updates on research related to mental health treatments, including new drug developments, therapies, and clinical trials. It highlights advancements in understanding the mechanisms of mental illnesses and offers insights into the effectiveness of emerging treatment options.



Helpful Articles



PineRest

Pine Rest's Mental Health Awareness Article offers valuable insights into various mental health topics, focusing on raising awareness and reducing stigma. It features expert advice, coping strategies, and personal stories about mental health challenges. The article addresses a wide range of issues, from anxiety and depression to stress management and therapy options.



HarvardHealth

Harvard Health's mental health section provides expert advice and articles on a wide range of mental health topics. It covers conditions such as depression, anxiety, and stress, offering tips on prevention, treatment options, and strategies for maintaining mental well-being. The section also delves into the latest research and news related to mental health.



APA

The article from the American Psychological Association explores efforts to improve youth mental health, highlighting trends such as increased attention to mental health in schools, digital platforms for therapy, and the integration of mental health services in primary care. Experts emphasize the need for comprehensive approaches to address the growing mental health crisis among young people, including reducing stigma and providing better access to care.



WHO

The World Health Organization's fact sheet on adolescent mental health emphasizes the importance of addressing mental health issues in youth, as mental disorders often emerge during adolescence. It highlights the global impact of conditions like depression and anxiety, and the need for early intervention, supportive environments, and access to mental health services. The document also stresses the importance of reducing stigma and creating policies that promote mental well-being for adolescents.
